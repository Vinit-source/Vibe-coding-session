So, Where Did This "Vibe Coding" Thing Come From?

The term was dreamed up in early 2025 by AI wizard Andrej Karpathy. He imagined a world where you could just "fully give in to the vibes... and forget that the code even exists," basically telling an AI what you want instead of typing semicolons.

It's the real-world version of his 2023 prediction that "the hottest new programming language is English." You talk, it codes. Simple as that.
